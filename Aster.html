History of Aster

The aster flower originates from Greek mythology, where the goddess Astraea transformed into a star and landed on Earth. The flower symbolizes patience, elegance, and love, making it a popular choice for various occasions. With its vibrant hues and delicate petals, the aster has become a beloved bloom in gardens and floral arrangements.
